Smoky Vegan Corn Chowder with Potatoes

Prep: 15 min | Cook: 30 min

Key Ingredients:

  • Fresh corn kernels
  • Yukon gold potatoes
  • Smoked paprika
  • Coconut milk
  • Vegetable broth

Step-by-Step Instructions:

  1. Heat olive oil in a large pot over medium heat. Add chopped onion and sauté until translucent (about 5 minutes).
  2. Add minced garlic and smoked paprika, stirring constantly for 1 minute until fragrant.
  3. Add diced potatoes and corn kernels, stirring to coat with the spices.
  4. Pour in vegetable broth and bring to a boil. Reduce heat and simmer for 20 minutes until potatoes are tender.
  5. Stir in coconut milk and use an immersion blender to partially blend the soup, leaving some chunks for texture.
  6. Season with salt, pepper, and a squeeze of fresh lime juice. Simmer for 5 more minutes.
  7. Serve hot, garnished with fresh cilantro and a sprinkle of smoked paprika.

Cheesy Vegan Broccoli & Barley with White Beans

Prep: 10 min | Cook: 25 min

Key Ingredients:

  • Pearl barley
  • Broccoli florets
  • Nutritional yeast
  • Cannellini beans
  • Cashews (for cheese sauce)

Step-by-Step Instructions:

  1. Cook barley according to package directions until tender (about 25 minutes).
  2. While barley cooks, steam broccoli florets for 5-7 minutes until bright green and tender-crisp.
  3. For the cheese sauce: Blend soaked cashews, nutritional yeast, lemon juice, garlic powder, and water until smooth and creamy.
  4. In a large skillet, heat olive oil and sauté minced garlic for 1 minute until fragrant.
  5. Add cooked barley, steamed broccoli, white beans, and the cheese sauce to the skillet.
  6. Stir everything together over medium heat until well combined and heated through (about 5 minutes).
  7. Season with salt, pepper, and a pinch of red pepper flakes for a subtle kick.
  8. Serve warm, garnished with extra nutritional yeast and fresh parsley.

30-Minute Easy Lentil Bolognese

Prep: 10 min | Cook: 20 min

Key Ingredients:

  • Brown lentils
  • Crushed tomatoes
  • Carrots, celery, onion
  • Italian herbs
  • Whole wheat pasta

Step-by-Step Instructions:

  1. Heat olive oil in a large saucepan over medium heat. Add finely chopped onion, carrot, and celery (the "soffritto").
  2. Sauté vegetables for 8-10 minutes until softened and fragrant.
  3. Add minced garlic, dried oregano, and basil, stirring for 1 minute until aromatic.
  4. Stir in cooked brown lentils and crushed tomatoes. Add a splash of vegetable broth if needed.
  5. Bring to a simmer, then reduce heat and cook uncovered for 15 minutes, stirring occasionally.
  6. While sauce simmers, cook your favorite pasta according to package directions.
  7. Season the lentil bolognese with salt, pepper, and a pinch of sugar to balance acidity.
  8. Serve sauce over cooked pasta, garnished with fresh basil and vegan parmesan if desired.

Farro Chickpea Skillet with Tomatoes, Kale, Cardamom & Lime

Prep: 15 min | Cook: 25 min

Key Ingredients:

  • Farro
  • Chickpeas
  • Cherry tomatoes
  • Kale
  • Cardamom

Step-by-Step Instructions:

  1. Cook farro according to package directions until tender but chewy (about 25 minutes).
  2. While farro cooks, heat olive oil in a large skillet over medium heat. Add sliced onions and sauté until softened.
  3. Add ground cardamom and cumin to the skillet, stirring for 30 seconds until fragrant.
  4. Add halved cherry tomatoes and cook for 5 minutes until they start to break down and release juices.
  5. Stir in chickpeas and cooked farro, mixing well to combine all ingredients.
  6. Add chopped kale and cover the skillet for 3-4 minutes until kale wilts.
  7. Remove from heat and stir in fresh lime juice, lime zest, and chopped cilantro.
  8. Season with salt and pepper. Serve warm as a main dish or hearty side.
