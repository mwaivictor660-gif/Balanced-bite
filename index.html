Smoky Vegan Corn Chowder with Potatoes

A rich, creamy comfort soup with smoky undertones.

45 mins 4 servings
Comfort Food Dinner

Ingredients

  • 4 cups fresh corn kernels
  • 3 medium yellow potatoes, cubed
  • 1 medium yellow onion, diced
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 stalks celery, diced
  • 1 can full-fat coconut milk
  • 3 cups vegetable broth
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 1¬Ω tsp smoked paprika
  • ¬Ω tsp ground cumin
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh chives for garnish

Instructions

  1. Prep vegetables: Peel and cube potatoes into ¬Ω-inch pieces. Dice onion and celery finely. Mince garlic.
  2. Build flavor base: Heat olive oil in a large pot over medium heat. Add onion and celery, cook 5-6 minutes until soft and fragrant.
  3. Add spices: Add garlic, smoked paprika, and cumin. Stir for 1 minute to bloom the spices.
  4. Cook potatoes: Add potatoes and vegetable broth. Bring to boil, then simmer 15-18 minutes until potatoes are tender.
  5. Add corn: Stir in corn kernels and cook for 5 minutes until tender.
  6. Make it creamy: Pour in coconut milk and simmer 5 minutes. For extra creaminess, blend one-third of the soup.
  7. Season and serve: Add salt and pepper to taste. Serve hot with fresh chives on top.

Cheesy Vegan Broccoli & Barley with White Beans

A hearty, protein-packed bowl with creamy cheese sauce.

45 mins 4 servings
High-Protein Lunch

Ingredients

  • 1 cup pearl barley
  • 4 cups broccoli florets
  • 1 can white beans, drained
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • ¬Ω cup nutritional yeast
  • 1 cup vegetable broth
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 2 tbsp lemon juice
  • 1 tbsp dijon mustard
  • Salt, pepper, red pepper flakes
  • Fresh parsley for garnish

Instructions

  1. Cook barley: Boil 3 cups water with salt. Add barley, reduce heat, and simmer 25-30 minutes until tender. Drain and set aside.
  2. Steam broccoli: Steam broccoli florets for 4-5 minutes until bright green and tender-crisp. Set aside.
  3. Saut√© garlic: Heat olive oil in a large skillet. Add garlic and cook 1 minute until fragrant.
  4. Add beans and barley: Add white beans and cooked barley. Toss together for 2 minutes.
  5. Make cheese sauce: Pour in vegetable broth and nutritional yeast. Stir until creamy sauce forms. Add lemon juice and mustard.
  6. Combine and serve: Fold in broccoli. Season with salt, pepper, and red pepper flakes. Garnish with parsley.

30-Minute Easy Lentil Bolognese

A quick, hearty pasta sauce the whole family loves.

30 mins 4 servings
Quick Meal Family Favorite

Ingredients

  • 1 cup red lentils, rinsed
  • 1 can crushed tomatoes
  • 3 tbsp tomato paste
  • 1 onion, finely diced
  • 2 carrots, finely diced
  • 2 celery stalks, finely diced
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 cups vegetable broth
  • 3 tbsp olive oil
  • 1¬Ω tsp dried oregano
  • 1 tsp dried basil
  • Salt, pepper, bay leaf
  • 400g spaghetti

Instructions

  1. Start pasta water: Fill a large pot with salted water and bring to boil while making sauce.
  2. Build the base: Heat olive oil in a large pot. Add onion, carrots, and celery. Cook 6-7 minutes until soft.
  3. Add aromatics: Add garlic and tomato paste. Cook 1-2 minutes until paste darkens slightly.
  4. Add lentils and liquid: Stir in rinsed lentils, crushed tomatoes, broth, and bay leaf. Bring to boil.
  5. Season and simmer: Add oregano, basil, salt, and pepper. Reduce heat and simmer 18-20 minutes until lentils are soft and sauce is thick.
  6. Cook pasta and serve: Cook spaghetti until al dente. Remove bay leaf from sauce. Serve sauce over pasta with fresh basil.

Farro Chickpea Skillet with Cardamom & Lime

An aromatic grain bowl with unique, incredible flavors.

45 mins 4 servings
High-Protein Unique Flavors

Ingredients

  • 1 cup farro
  • 1 can chickpeas, drained
  • 2 cups cherry tomatoes, halved
  • 4 cups kale, chopped
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 red onion, sliced
  • 3 tbsp olive oil
  • Juice of 2 limes
  • Zest of 1 lime
  • ¬æ tsp ground cardamom
  • ¬Ω tsp cumin
  • Salt, pepper, red pepper flakes
  • Fresh cilantro for garnish

Instructions

  1. Cook farro: Boil 3 cups salted water. Add farro and simmer 25-30 minutes until tender but chewy. Drain and set aside.
  2. Make dressing: Mix lime juice, zest, cardamom, cumin, and coriander in a small bowl. Set aside.
  3. Crisp chickpeas: Heat 2 tbsp olive oil in a large skillet. Add dried chickpeas and cook 5-6 minutes until golden and crispy.
  4. Add aromatics: Push chickpeas aside. Add remaining oil, red onion, and garlic. Cook 3-4 minutes.
  5. Add tomatoes and kale: Add cherry tomatoes and cook 3-4 minutes until softened. Add kale and cook 2-3 minutes until wilted.
  6. Combine and finish: Add cooked farro and toss. Pour dressing over everything. Season with salt, pepper, and red pepper flakes. Top with fresh cilantro and lime wedges.
